Our Story

We're Driftocity — an independent digital products brand that builds tools and guides for people who don't have time to waste.

Driftocity exists because most software is over-engineered and most guides are padded thin. We set out to fix both of those things — one product at a time.

We build AI-powered SaaS tools that solve real problems: writing business plans, generating project estimates, analyzing trades. And we write PDF guides that actually go deep — not 10 pages of obvious advice, but dense, practical frameworks built from hard-won experience.

Everything we make is designed to deliver value immediately. You buy, you access, you use. No onboarding calls, no account setup headaches, no waiting.

We operate under the Driftocity umbrella alongside Hockersmith Consulting, our AI business consulting arm that helps companies implement automation and custom AI agents.
